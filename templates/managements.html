Management Desk

Ilahia Law College is managed by Ilahia Trust. The trust is the noble dream of a group of visionaries hailing from diverse socio-economic, political, cultural and business circuits in and around Muvattupuzha that came true in the year 1992. Ever since, the Trust has been catering to the educational aspirations of the region. The Trust is steered by a 13-member governing board and its prime objective is to attend to the educational needs of the knowledge seekers located in and around its place of origin. Now Ilahia Trust has 70 members, of which 13 members are elected to the executive committee. The establishment of a lower primary school (Kavumkara Muslim L.P. School) in 1992 was an affirmation of its phenomenal growth. The commencement of a prestigious English medium School (of CBSE Syllabus) named Ilahia Public School in the same year added another landmark to its timeline of achievements.
