Academic Regulations & Campus Guidelines

Ilahia Law College maintains high standards of academic excellence and campus discipline. Below are the key regulations all students must follow.

Academic Policies

Attendance Requirements
  • Minimum 75% attendance mandatory
  • Medical leave requires documentation
Examination System
Semester Exams
  • 10 semesters (5-year program)
  • 6 semesters (3-year program)
Internal Assessment
  • 2 internal exams
  • 1 presentation
  • 1 assignment

Campus Discipline

College Hours
  • 9:30 AM - 3:30 PM
  • Latecomers may face disciplinary action
Dress Code
Male Students
  • Black pants + White shirt
  • College badge mandatory
Female Students
  • Black pants + White shirt
  • Black blazer + College badge

Facility Regulations

  • Library Rules

    Mandatory participation • Graded activity • Court etiquette required

Anti-Ragging

Zero tolerance policy • Immediate suspension • Legal action

Anti-Harassment

Confidential reporting • Committee support • Strict penalties

Grievance Redressal

Approach committee members directly • Written complaints preferred • Resolution within 14 days

General Guidelines

Communication

Check notice boards daily • Monitor college emails • Follow social media

Fee Policy

No fee clearance = No promotion • Payment deadlines strict • Installment options available

Final Authority

Principal's decisions final • Rules subject to change • Management reserves rights
