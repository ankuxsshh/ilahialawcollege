Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

The IQAC at Ilahia Law College is committed to maintaining academic and administrative excellence. Following NAAC guidelines, we strive for continuous improvement, enhancing education quality and institutional effectiveness.

OUR MISSION

To provide an academically rigorous legal education that equips students with the skills, knowledge, and values necessary for success in the legal profession.

To create a dynamic learning environment that encourages critical thinking, research, and intellectual engagement.

To offer opportunities for practical learning through internships, moot courts, legal clinics, and community service projects.

To instill a deep sense of social responsibility and ethical conduct, empowering students to contribute positively to society through law.

OUR VISION

To become a nationally recognized institution, upholding highest quality standards, fostering innovation, and producing globally competent, socially responsible individuals.

Functions of IQAC


The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) at Ilahia Law College is expected to perform the following key functions to ensure quality education and continuous improvement :

Develop Benchmarks

Set standards for academic and administrative activities to ensure excellence.

Learner-Centric

Promote student growth and participatory learning through quality education.

Feedback System

Collect and analyze feedback from stakeholders to improve institutional quality.

Dissemination

Share best practices and quality insights with the academic community.

Workshops

Organize seminars and workshops on quality-related themes for faculty/staff.

Documentation

Record activities and programs that contribute to quality improvement.

Nodal Agency

Coordinate all quality initiatives and act as a catalyst for improvement.

Database System

Maintain MIS for decision-making and performance tracking.

AQAR Report

By performing these functions, the IQAC plays a vital role in ensuring Ilahia Law College's commitment to delivering high-quality education, fostering innovation, and achieving academic excellence.

IQAC Strategies

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) at Ilahia Law College adopts the following strategies to enhance performance and ensure quality outcomes:

  • Timely, efficient, and progressive performance of academic, administrative, and financial tasks

  • Relevance and quality of academic and research programmes

  • Equitable access and affordability of academic programmes for various sections of society

  • Optimization and integration of modern teaching and learning methods

  • Credibility of evaluation procedures

  • Adequate maintenance and functioning of support structures and services

  • Research sharing and networking with institutions in India and abroad
