About Course

The 5-year Integrated BA.LLB. program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive legal education while simultaneously earning a Bachelor’s degree in Law. This program follows the guidelines set by the Bar Council of India (BCI) under the Advocates Act, 1961, ensuring that graduates are eligible for enrolment as Advocates upon successful completion.

This program spans five years, integrating academic learning with practical legal training. The curriculum builds a strong foundation in legal studies and the chosen undergraduate discipline while incorporating the case study method of teaching, legal workshops, and exposure to national, international, and interstate seminars. In the final six months, students undergo mandatory practical training, gaining hands-on experience in legal practice.

Eligibility & Admission Criteria


  • (i) Nativity Requirements:

    • The applicant must be an Indian citizen.
    • Only candidates of Kerala origin are eligible for reservation benefits or fee concessions.

    To establish Indian citizenship, upload any one of the following:

    • Birth certificate from competent authority showing place of birth in India.
    • Relevant SSLC certificate page showing place of birth in India.
    • Valid Indian passport.
    • Certificate from Village Officer/competent authority verifying Indian nativity (prescribed format).
  • Proof of Kerala Origin (for claiming reservation or fee concession):

    • Relevant SSLC certificate page showing place of birth in Kerala.
    • Birth certificate showing Kerala as place of birth.
    • Passport of candidate or parent showing place of birth in Kerala.
      (Include relationship certificate if parent's passport is submitted.)
    • SSLC certificate page of candidate or parent showing Kerala as place of birth.
      (Include relationship certificate if parent's certificate is submitted.)
    • Village Officer/Tahsildar certificate confirming birth in Kerala (prescribed format).
  • (ii) Academic Qualifications:

    • Passed Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) of Kerala Board or equivalent recognized by Kerala Universities.
    • Equivalent 10+2 qualification from recognized Central/State educational boards.
    • Distance/correspondence 10+2 or First Degree certificate holders are eligible.
    • Candidates with open university degrees without basic qualification are not eligible.
    • Appearing candidates may apply, subject to final certificate submission at admission.
    • Minimum 45% aggregate marks for General category; 40% for SC/ST candidates.
    • Rounding off marks is not permitted.
  • (iii) Age Criteria:

    • Minimum age: 17 years as on December 31 of admission year.
    • Upper age limit subject to Hon’ble Supreme Court decision.
    • If BCI age rule under Clause 28 is reinstated, only eligible candidates can take the entrance exam.

Fees and Funding

Scholarships and financial aid are available for meritorious students. The course fee covers tuition, library access, and internship programs.
